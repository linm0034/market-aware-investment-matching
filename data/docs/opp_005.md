# High Yield Bond Fund
**Type:** Fund  
**Risk Level:** 4/5  
**Liquidity/Lock-up:** Typically 90 days lock-up or settlement constraints.

## Investment Objective
Designed for clients seeking capital preservation aligned with a high risk appetite.

## How It Works
The fund allocates across US Treasuries ladder. Returns are driven by structured payoff conditions and underlying index paths.

## Suitable For
- Clients with goal: Growth
- Horizon: medium term (1–3 years)
- Risk tolerance: 3–4

## Key Risks
- Market risk, NAV fluctuations
- credit risk and spread volatility

## Not Suitable For
- Clients with short horizon or low risk tolerance

## Fees
Management/ongoing fees may apply (approx. 0.01).
