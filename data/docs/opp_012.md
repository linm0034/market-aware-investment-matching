# Gold Defensive Allocation Fund
**Type:** Fund  
**Risk Level:** 3/5  
**Liquidity/Lock-up:** Typically 14 days lock-up or settlement constraints.

## Investment Objective
Designed for clients seeking income aligned with a moderate risk appetite.

## How It Works
The fund allocates across short-duration investment grade bonds. Returns are driven by coupon carry and credit spreads.

## Suitable For
- Clients with goal: Balanced Growth
- Horizon: short-to-medium term (6–24 months)
- Risk tolerance: 2–3

## Key Risks
- Market risk, NAV fluctuations
- credit risk and spread volatility

## Not Suitable For
- Clients with seeking aggressive growth or unwilling to accept NAV fluctuations

## Fees
Management/ongoing fees may apply (approx. 0.009).
