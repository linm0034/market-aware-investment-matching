The fund allocates across investment grade corporate bonds. Returns are driven by equity dividends and quality factor.

## Suitable For
- Clients with goal: Growth
- Horizon: medium term (1–3 years)
- Risk tolerance: 3–4

## Key Risks
- Market risk, NAV fluctuations
- credit risk and spread volatility

## Not Suitable For
