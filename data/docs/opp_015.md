# REITs Income Fund
**Type:** Fund  
**Risk Level:** 3/5  
**Liquidity/Lock-up:** Typically 60 days lock-up or settlement constraints.

## Investment Objective
Designed for clients seeking growth aligned with a moderate risk appetite.

## How It Works
The fund allocates across global dividend equities. Returns are driven by credit spread compression/expansion.

## Suitable For
- Clients with goal: Balanced Growth
- Horizon: medium term (1–3 years)
- Risk tolerance: 2–3

## Key Risks
- Market risk, NAV fluctuations
- credit risk and spread volatility

## Not Suitable For
- Clients with seeking aggressive growth or unwilling to accept NAV fluctuations

## Fees
Management/ongoing fees may apply (approx. 0.01).
