# Low Volatility Equity Fund
**Type:** Fund  
**Risk Level:** 2/5  
**Liquidity/Lock-up:** Typically 30 days lock-up or settlement constraints.

## Investment Objective
Designed for clients seeking growth aligned with a low risk appetite.

## How It Works
The fund allocates across gold and defensive assets. Returns are driven by equity dividends and quality factor.

## Suitable For
- Clients with goal: Income / Preservation
- Horizon: short-to-medium term (6–24 months)
- Risk tolerance: 1–2

## Key Risks
- Market risk, NAV fluctuations
- inflation / reinvestment risk

## Not Suitable For
- Clients with seeking aggressive growth or unwilling to accept NAV fluctuations

## Fees
Management/ongoing fees may apply (approx. 0.008).
